Cool Bank gpt

This site is a demo to help USAA employees to consider the potential benefits of incorporating Generative Pre-trained Transformer aka GPT models into their workflows.

The chat agent has 2 buttons.
The Talk button sends your query directly to OpenAI's WebGPT API and responds as a support agent.
The Research button sends the query first to a custom search engine specifically searching the public facing views of usaa.com and related domains. Then that data is fed into the api along with the original query. Finally the model is requested to reflect on the answer it gave and give additional insights.

This site does not have access to any internal information at USAA and is only meant to be a demonstration. The goal would to be to build something like this tool internally to help us all do our jobs better.
